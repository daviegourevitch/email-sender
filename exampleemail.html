Welcome to Carleton Strategy Club!

Thanks for visiting us at the expo last week, we had so much interest! We are the Carleton University Strategy Club, or CUSC for short. We are a Carleton University's oldest and biggest gaming group. With a collection of hundreds of games, there's a ton to explore! You told us you were interested in - Board Games - RPGs - Gaming tournaments - Everything we have to offer! This Wednesday September 11 will be our first official board game night! Feel free to drop by anytime from 5:30 pm - 11:00 pm to hang out, have some snacks and play our games. We meet near the Tim Hortons in University Centre. Follow the signs to find our space or check out our Facebook page for a map.

Dungeons and Dragons Intro Night

This Friday September 13th will be our annual D&D intro night. Never played before? Always wanted to? Just want an excuse to kill goblins? Come join us for an action packed night of fun and adventure! As always, the event will be totally free! We will be explaining how to play and making characters from 6:30 to 7:30. If you already know how to play, feel free to arrive at 7:30 when everyone will be assigned a table and play will begin. For more information, or to RSVP, please check out our Facebook Event.
